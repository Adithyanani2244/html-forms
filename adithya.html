ACTOR:

Anand Devarakonda

Anand Devarkonda (born March 15 ,1992) is an indian actor ,who appears in Telugu films. Younger brother of Vijay Devarakonda. Deverakonda was born in Hyderabad of present-day Telangana, India to Govardhan Rao and his wife Madhavi.Govardhan Rao was a TV serial director while Madhavi was a soft-skills tutor. His brother Vijay Deverakonda, is also an actor in Telugu films. Anand Deverakonda completed his engineering and was placed in Deloitte.He worked in US for a while and returned to India to pursue a full time career in films.

his films include Baby[1], Pushpaka_Vimanam[2], Middle_Class_Melodies[3],Dorasaani[4]. Deverakonda made his film debut in 2019 with Dorasaani, a periodic love story directed by KVR Mahendra and starring Shivatmika Rajashekar. Although the film didn't do well commercially, Deverakonda went onto win Debut Male award at Zee Cine Awards Telugu 2020.
